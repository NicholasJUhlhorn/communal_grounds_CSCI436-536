About Communal Grounds

Mission Statement

Our mission is to cultivate a vibrant, collaborative ecosystem where individuals can connect with diverse projects, share skills, and turn ideas into reality. We believe that the best work happens when people from different backgrounds come together to build, learn, and grow as a community. Communal Grounds is where projects get rooted and ideas flourish.

Code of Conduct

Communal Grounds is dedicated to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone! We do not tolerate harassment in any form. Our core values include:

  • Be Respectful: Treat all members with kindness and professionalism.
  • Be Inclusive: Welcome new ideas and new members. Everyone started somewhere.
  • Be Thoughtful: Ensure your contributions and feedback are constructive.
  • Be Safe: Report any unwelcome behavior immediately to the site administrators.

Failure to adhere to the Code of Conduct may result in temporary or permanent suspension from the platform.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Who can join a project?

   A: Anyone! Our platform is designed to be accessible to all skill levels. If a project is public, you can hit the "Apply" button on the project card. The project creator will review your request.

Q: How do I track my project's status?

   A: Every project has a progress bar on its card, which is manually updated by the project owner to reflect its status percentage.

Q: Can I create a private project?

   A: Yes, all projects start as private and are only visible to collaborators. You can choose to make a project public to seek new members on the Home page.
